Messaging Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 | Aaron Coffey — SMS Automation Services for Local Businesses

Introduction

This Messaging Privacy Policy describes how Aaron Coffey ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects information related to our SMS and text messaging services provided on behalf of local service businesses.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our messaging services, we may collect the following information:

  • Your phone number (provided via caller ID when you place a call, or when you submit a contact form)
  • Your name (if provided by you during a conversation or form submission)
  • The content of text messages you send and receive
  • Date and time of messages and calls

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your missed calls with a follow-up text message
  • To respond to inquiries submitted through website contact forms
  • To send service-related follow-up messages such as appointment confirmations
  • To send review requests after a completed service
  • To provide customer support when you reply to our messages

Sharing of Information

All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your personal information, mobile phone number, or messaging opt-in consent data with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Your information is used solely to facilitate the messaging services described in this policy.

Data Retention

We retain message data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP
  • Request information about the data we have collected about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our messaging practices, please contact Aaron Coffey by replying HELP to any message or by emailing us at the address provided in our text messages.

Messaging Terms of Service

Last updated: April 2026

Program Description

Our messaging service, operated by Aaron Coffey, provides automated text message communications on behalf of local service businesses. These messages include missed call follow-ups, responses to contact form inquiries, appointment scheduling, and service review requests.

Consent and Opt-In

By placing a phone call to a participating business, submitting a contact form on a business website, or providing your phone number at the time of service, you consent to receive text messages related to your inquiry or service experience. Consent is not required as a condition of purchasing any goods or services. Full details of our consent collection methods are documented on our Consent Documentation page.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions. Missed call follow-ups typically include 1 initial message. Contact form responses may include up to 4 follow-up messages. Review requests include 1 initial message and up to 1 reminder.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out and stop receiving messages. You will receive a single confirmation message acknowledging your opt-out request. No further messages will be sent.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance. You will receive a message with contact information for customer support.

Carrier Liability

Carriers are not liable for any delayed or undelivered messages.

Supported Carriers

Our messaging services are compatible with all major US wireless carriers.

Privacy

Please refer to our Messaging Privacy Policy above for information about how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Contact

For questions or concerns regarding these terms, reply HELP to any message or contact Aaron Coffey via the information provided in our text messages.
